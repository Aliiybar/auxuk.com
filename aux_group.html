AUX Group

Established in 1986, AUX Group covers several sectors: air conditioning, energy utilization, power distribution, renewable energy and medical services. For many years, it has been among the top 500 enterprises in China.

The AUX Group has more than 30,000 employees and has 14 production sites around the world, as well as 6 research and development (R&D) centers.
